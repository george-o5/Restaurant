Our Heritage

For over 30 years, George's family has brought authentic Tamil Nadu non-vegetarian cuisine . From Chettinad pepper chicken to Madurai mutton fry, each dish honors traditional recipes perfected across generations. Our spices are hand-ground daily, and every curry simmers to order in the same iron pots my grandfather used in his original 1990s kitchen. This is unfiltered Tamil Nadu flavor - no compromises, no shortcuts, just honest food with character as bold as our community..
